2001/11/06 		The Academic Researcher

This actor is likely to be interested a variety of notions regarding sail flight in the inner solar system. They may be looking for optimization ideas, science value for a particular flight, or something apparently unrelated to solar sailing. It is very likely that every feature of the application should be open to this actor unless there is a really good reason why it should not be.

The Celestial Mechanics/Navigation Student

This actor is interested in the application as a learning tool. Features of the application that deliver a 'student' experience are most likely to be useful. Some of the automation of simulations and the related resources will probably not be needed.

The Solar Sail Engineer

This actor is similar to the academic researcher except that they only care about the nuts and bolts of sail flights. Tangential topics will probably be of little interest. This actor is most likely going to be the first to notice bugs and resource bottlenecks. They are unlikely to care all that much about how the application does what it does.

The SailAway Librarian

It is possible for this actor may also be an 'Academic Researcher' or an inanimate remote library. Each possibility is listed below but will be treated the same by the system.

This actor is the one who cares to see that research is available to the community with little duplication. The librarian will be the one to provide resources for the collection of data from previous simulations for browsing by future users.

This actor represents an internet resource willing to act as a remote datastore. This datastore will function much like a local library, but may do so with a completely differrent set of business rules and an external application.

The Notification Tool

This actor is an application resposible for accepting input from SailAway and getting it to the user requesting it. It may be something as simple as an email outlet or something more complex like a web page generator or file handling script.

The 'Uneducated' Supporter

This actor is the person who wants to support the research of other users, but does not feel they have the background to do the research themselves. This actor is most likely going to provide computing resources, time, and/or money to the efforts of others.

The Simulation Clock

This actor represents the external clock that may be used by a list of commands provided by another actor when the System is to be run without direct interaction.
